How to make Spicy Cream Pasta

Name: Seon Ui Lee Student ID: 2076301

Ingredient

  • Seasoning: salt, pepper, butter, peperoncion
  • spaghetti, bacon, onion, galic, egg
  • milk, cream
  • pot, frying pan


Recipe

  1. Boil the spaghetti in water. Add salt.
  2. Slice the bacon(4 strips), one onion, more than 5 cloves of galics. And stir-fry them on frying pan.
  3. Add boiled sphaghetti
  4. Pour milk and cream
  5. Mix and stir. Simmer them. Add crashed peperoncion
  6. Turn off the gas, and add yolk. Have it.
